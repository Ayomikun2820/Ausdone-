AUSDONE PERSONAL SUPPORT

We offer a full range of services

We understand every family is different and we offer a full range to support you and your family. We have an amazing team of carers that are always happy to assist when needed.

Personal care

Do you need help getting in and out of bed, bathing or getting dressed and looking sharp? We can help support

✓ Showering, bathing,

✓ Hygiene and grooming

✓ Podiatry, physio and other therapies

✓ Nursing, wound care and medications

✓ Help with impairments or continence

✓ Mobility & exercise assistance

Household assistance

We can do your household chores and help with many daily tasks or just provide companionship

✓ Cleaning, laundry and dishwashing

✓ Other household chores

✓ Home or garden maintenance

✓ Changes to home e.g, ramps or hand rails

✓ Meals and food preparation

✓ TV, movies and games

Transport & mobility

All of our caregivers have a drivers license and can assist in nyway they can

✓ Medical appointments

✓ Aids to stsay independent

✓ Social outings, groups and hosting visitors

✓ Transport and taxi vouchers

✓ Help paying bills

✓ Grocery shopping and errands
